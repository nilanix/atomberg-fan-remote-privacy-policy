Our app does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information. The application works purely as a fan remote control and does not require user registration or personal data.

The app may request certain permissions (Infrared, Bluetooth, or Internet) only to enable the core functionality of controlling the fan. These permissions are not used to collect personal data.

Learn more about Google’s data practices here: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since the app does not request personal data, it is safe for all users.
